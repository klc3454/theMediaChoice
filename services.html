Interactive and Targeted Internet Media Tours

An Internet Media Tour (IMT) is a powerful tool which helps organizations capitalize on the fast-growing popularity reach of the internet. A typical IMT uses a 3rd-party or company spokesperson who engages in a series of interviews with online media outlets. The specialization and number of web sites allows clients to target specific audiences and deliver messages directly to the end-users.

Radio Media Tours

A Radio Media Tour (RMT) is series of radio interviews with stations around the country. The interview features a 3rd party or company spokesperson who delivers a pre-conceived message packaged with a news angle.

Audio News Releases

An Audio News Release (ANR) is a 60-second scripted and voiced radio story that contains one or more spokesperson. It is pitched and distributed to a targeted list stations and networks nationwide via internet download or audio disc.
