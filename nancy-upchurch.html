Nancy Blair Upchurch

Nancy Blair Upchurch is co-founder and vice-president of The Media Choice, Inc. Her past experience includes work in television news as both an on-air reporter for ABC and CBS stations and producer/writer for the Fox affiliate in Atlanta.
Nancy Blair has worked in various PR agencies generating press releases, media alerts and public relations campaigns giving her a vantage point outside the newsroom. Here she fine tuned her skill of creating and pitching ideas that were both newsworthy and met the clients particular set of expectations and goals. She also used her news background to media train clients for upcoming television and radio interviews and her writing experience to produce and execute corporate videos.
Nancy Blair was born and bred in East Tennessee and received a Bachelor's degree from the University of Georgia School of Journalism. She is our company go-to person for editing scripts and pulling together project details - a skill she honed as the "activity director" for her family.
